Acceptable Use Policy

Guidelines for responsible use of PrismaTech services.

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of PrismaTech's services and platform. By using our services, you agree to comply with this policy and all applicable laws and regulations.

2. Permitted Uses

You may use PrismaTech services to:

  • Create legitimate web applications for personal or business use
  • Develop educational or informational content
  • Build applications that comply with all applicable laws
  • Create applications that respect intellectual property rights
  • Develop applications that promote positive user experiences

3. Prohibited Uses

You may not use PrismaTech services to:

3.1 Illegal Activities

  • Create applications that facilitate illegal activities
  • Develop software that violates any local, state, national, or international law
  • Create applications that promote violence or harm to others
  • Develop tools for hacking, phishing, or other malicious activities

3.2 Harmful Content

  • Create applications containing malware, viruses, or other harmful code
  • Develop applications that collect personal information without consent
  • Create applications that promote discrimination or hate speech
  • Develop applications that facilitate harassment or bullying

3.3 Intellectual Property Violations

  • Create applications that infringe on copyrights, trademarks, or patents
  • Develop applications that use copyrighted material without permission
  • Create applications that reverse engineer or copy existing software

3.4 System Abuse

  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems
  • Use automated tools to abuse or overload our services
  • Interfere with the proper functioning of our platform
  • Attempt to circumvent security measures or access controls

4. Content Guidelines

All content created using PrismaTech services must:

  • Be original or properly licensed
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respect the rights and dignity of others
  • Not contain false or misleading information
  • Be appropriate for the intended audience

5. Privacy and Data Protection

When creating applications that handle personal data, you must:

  • Comply with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Implement appropriate security measures
  • Obtain necessary consents from users
  • Provide clear privacy notices
  • Handle data responsibly and securely

6. Commercial Use

Commercial use of applications created with PrismaTech is permitted, provided that:

  • All applicable laws and regulations are followed
  • Intellectual property rights are respected
  • Users are not misled about the nature of the application
  • Fair business practices are maintained

7. Enforcement

We reserve the right to:

  • Monitor usage of our services
  • Investigate reports of policy violations
  • Suspend or terminate accounts that violate this policy
  • Remove content that violates this policy
  • Report illegal activities to appropriate authorities

8. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this Acceptable Use Policy, please report it to us immediately at support@prismatech.co.uk. Include as much detail as possible about the violation.

9. Consequences of Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Warning notifications
  • Temporary suspension of services
  • Permanent termination of accounts
  • Legal action where appropriate

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Acceptable Use Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@prismatech.co.uk
