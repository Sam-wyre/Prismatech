Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PrismaTech and our AI-powered web development platform.

What is PrismaTech?

PrismaTech is an AI-powered platform that transforms natural language prompts into fully functional web applications. Simply describe what you want to build, and our AI creates a beautiful, responsive website for you in minutes.

How does PrismaTech work?

Our process is simple:

  • Describe your vision in plain English
  • Our AI analyzes your requirements
  • We generate a customized web application
  • You can customize and deploy your app

When will PrismaTech be available?

We're currently in development and building our waitlist. Join our waitlist to be the first to know when we launch and get early access to our platform.

Do I need coding experience to use PrismaTech?

No coding experience required! PrismaTech is designed for everyone, from complete beginners to experienced developers. Our AI handles all the technical complexity, so you can focus on your ideas.

What types of web applications can I create?

You can create a wide variety of applications including:

  • Business websites and landing pages
  • E-commerce stores
  • Portfolio websites
  • Blogs and content sites
  • SaaS applications
  • Restaurant and service websites

How much will PrismaTech cost?

We're still finalizing our pricing structure. Join our waitlist to be the first to know about our launch pricing and special early adopter offers.

Can I customize the generated applications?

Yes! While our AI creates the initial application, you'll have full control to customize colors, content, layout, and functionality to match your specific needs and brand.

Will my applications be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All applications created with PrismaTech are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

What about SEO and performance?

Our AI automatically implements SEO best practices and performance optimizations, including fast loading times, proper meta tags, and search engine optimization.

Can I integrate with third-party services?

Yes! PrismaTech supports integration with popular services like payment processors, email marketing tools, analytics platforms, and social media APIs.

Is there customer support available?

We provide comprehensive customer support including documentation, tutorials, and direct support via email. Our team is committed to helping you succeed.

How do I join the waitlist?

Simply enter your email address in the waitlist form on our homepage. We'll notify you as soon as PrismaTech is ready for launch!
